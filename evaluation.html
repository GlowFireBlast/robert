5. Evaluation

In terms of the development process, I found that using a structured approach was very helpful. I followed the design and development stages closely to my plan, which allowed me to stay organized and focused throughout the project. I also found that testing the app routinely was crucial in identifying and fixing bugs early on in the development process.

Overall, I am very happy with the final artefact. It meets all the requirements for the brief and is a useful, innovative tool for campers. The user interface is quite accessible, and provides accurate logged information.

In terms of improvements, I would like to add more features to the embedded system, such as waterproof casing, improved power efficiency (e.g, solar power or rechargable batteries), and more sensors such as for humidity and air quality.
